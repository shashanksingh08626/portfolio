01 THE STORY

Why did this idea
come to life?

Every meaningful innovation begins with a simple question. While exploring the automobile service industry, I noticed recurring frustrations faced by vehicle owners—from expensive repairs to unreliable workshops and delayed assistance. Those observations eventually evolved into the vision behind AUTO AID.

THE SPARK
"What if... vehicle owners could access diagnostics, trusted workshops, emergency assistance and service history—all from one intelligent platform?"

That single question became the first step toward imagining AUTO AID.

High Repair Costs

Expensive repairs due to unorganized services and hidden charges.

Lack of Trust

Uncertainty about quality, transparency and genuine parts.

Poor EV Service

Limited availability of EV repair and charging solutions.

Emergency Delays

Slow response during breakdowns and roadside emergencies.

These challenges affect millions of vehicle owners every day.
